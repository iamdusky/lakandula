# LAKANDULA — Asset Specification (for commissioning)

Everything the game needs from an artist, composer, and voice talent to
replace the procedural placeholders. Current placeholders are generated by
`tools/gen_assets.gd` / `tools/gen_audio.gd` — run the game to see exactly
where each asset appears and at what size.

## Style guide

- **Setting:** the Visayas, April 1521. Pre-colonial Philippine material
  culture: nipa-and-bamboo architecture, karakoa/balangay watercraft, putong
  head-wraps, kampilan and kris blades, woven textiles, coral-stone kuta.
  Spanish expedition gear: morion helmets, tercio pike-and-shot, carracks.
  Research references: Boxer Codex illustrations, Visayan *pintados*
  tattooing, Butuan boat archaeology.
- **Rendering:** top-down-ish 3/4 view, pixel art, 64 px terrain grid.
  Readable at 100% zoom on a 1600×900 viewport; nearest-neighbor filtering
  (no anti-aliasing against transparency).
- **Palette anchors** (placeholders use these; artist may refine):
  Mactan red-orange `#C74D29`, Spanish steel `#5A5F6B`, gold `#E6BF33`,
  thatch `#CCAD66`, jungle `#295A2E`, sea `#1A476E`, sand `#D9BD7A`.
- **Faction readability rule:** every Mactan unit silhouette must read
  "warm + organic", every Spanish unit "cool + metallic" at 28 px.

## UI / misc

Selection ring (36×36), minimap faction dots supplied by code, projectiles
(arrow 14×4, cannonball 10×10, musket flash), particle sprites (smoke puff,
fire lick, splash droplet, blood/impact), game icon (128, 512, 1024), title
banner art for the main menu (1600×400, the strait at dawn).

## Audio

**Music** (loopable, OGG): calm exploration ~2 min — kulintang ensemble
(kulintang melody, agung bass, babandil timekeeper) over surf; battle
~90 s — add war drums, faster agung pulse; defeat/victory stings ~10 s.
Live kulintang recording strongly preferred over samples.

**SFX:** selection tap, move order, melee hit (wood/flesh ×3 variants),
arrow loose + impact, arquebus shot (with dry-click empty variant), cannon
broadside, ship creak, boarding clash, building fire, structure collapse,
tide bell, conversion chant, ritual chime, death cries (Mactan ×2,
Spanish ×2).

Also: Spanish barks for Magellan (period Castilian, 2–3 lines), generic
